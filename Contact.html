Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all products. Items must be in original condition with all accessories and packaging.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we ship within the United States. International shipping will be available soon.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this to track your package on our website.

Do you price match?

Yes! We offer price matching on identical products from authorized retailers. Contact us with the competitor's price.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and financing options through Klarna.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your personal and payment information.
